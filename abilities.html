A B I L I T I E S

WHISPER

passive

Jhin’s weapon, Whisper, is a highly modified firearm crafted by the infamous arms dealer Khada Jhin. Whisper is designed for high damage and precision, firing only four shots before needing to reload. The fourth shot is always a guaranteed critical strike, representing Jhin’s obsession with perfection and theatrics. This mechanic reflects his meticulous nature, as he believes in crafting the perfect kill, rather than relying on brute force.

DANCING GRENADE

Q

Jhin uses a small grenade as part of his arsenal, but not just any ordinary grenade. In line with his artistic nature, this grenade bounces between enemies, causing escalating damage, much like a performer’s crescendo. The grenade is a reflection of his love for orchestrating death, where each bounce brings the explosive sequence closer to its finale, symbolizing Jhin’s desire to create a symphony of destruction.

DEADLY FLOURISH

W

Deadly Flourish showcases Jhin's remarkable precision and his sniper-like skills. The ability shoots a long-range skillshot, and if it hits a marked target, it roots them. This marks his enemies for the perfect shot, as Jhin revels in making each shot count. The ability is a testament to his philosophy of killing with grace and artistry—striking only when the moment is perfect.

CAPTIVE AUDIENCE

E

Jhin places a lotus trap, a tool that reflects his desire for elaborate, theatrical executions. These traps are more than just a tool of war—they are a performance in themselves. When an enemy triggers the trap, it slows them before detonating in a mesmerizing yet deadly explosion, fitting Jhin’s vision of turning each kill into a masterpiece. This ability embodies his love for spectacle, as he creates a scene where his enemies are the unwilling actors.

CURTAIN CALL

R

Jhin’s ultimate is the culmination of his character: a theatrical finale where he reveals his true nature as an artist of death. Curtain Call is a global-range ability where Jhin carefully lines up four deadly shots, with the last one delivering devastating critical damage. The long range reflects his calm, calculating patience as a sniper, waiting for the perfect moment to finish his performance. Each shot is delivered with the precision of a virtuoso, and the slow, measured pace of the ability is akin to the final act of a grand opera.
